How Does Maply News Work?

  1. Aggregation: Our AI pulls news from hundreds of reputable sources worldwide, including social media platforms like Twitter and TikTok, as well as traditional news outlets.
  2. Analysis: The AI engine analyzes each news story for relevance, importance, and location. Critical alerts and breaking news are prioritized.
  3. Visualization: News stories are displayed as pins on the map, categorized by event type and location. You can zoom in for localized news or zoom out to get a global perspective.
  4. Customization: Refine your feed by applying filters for location, time, source, or event type. You can even search for specific terms to narrow down the stories that matter most to you.
